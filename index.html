Rice/ধান - Crop Information
Rice Cultivation Details in Bangladesh:
Aspect Details
Climate Tropical and subtropical, with high humidity and significant monsoon rainfall.
Ideal Temperature 20°C to 35°C
Humidity High humidity levels above 60% are ideal for rice cultivation.
Rainfall 150-300 cm annually, mainly provided by the monsoon season.
Soil Type Loamy to clayey soil with a pH range of 5.5 to 6.5 is best suited for rice.
Key Rice Seasons
  • Aman: July - December
  • Boro: November - April
  • Aus: March - August
Fertilizer Recommendations
  • NPK Fertilizer (10-20-10): Promotes growth and healthy grains.
  • Urea Fertilizer: Supports vegetative growth.
  • Organic Fertilizer: Improves soil fertility.
Irrigation Regular irrigation is essential during the dry season, especially for Boro rice.
Wheat/গম - Crop Information
Wheat Cultivation Details in Bangladesh:
Aspect Details
Climate Grows well in cool, dry conditions. Prefers dry winters with moderate rainfall.
Ideal Temperature 15°C to 25°C
Humidity Moderate humidity levels, below 50%, are ideal for wheat.
Rainfall 40-75 cm annually, less water-demanding compared to rice.
Soil Type Loamy soil with good drainage and a pH of 6.0 to 7.5 is best suited.
Key Wheat Season Planted in November and harvested between February and March.
Fertilizer Recommendations
  • NPK Fertilizer (12-32-16): Supports root growth and grain formation.
  • Potash Fertilizer: Enhances drought resistance.
Irrigation Minimal irrigation required compared to rice; supplemental water during dry periods is beneficial.
Corn/ভুট্টা - Crop Information
Corn Cultivation Details in Bangladesh:
Aspect Details
Climate Requires warm weather and moderate rainfall; thrives in tropical and subtropical conditions.
Ideal Temperature 20°C to 30°C
Humidity Moderate humidity is beneficial; prefers humidity levels between 50-80%.
Rainfall 75-150 cm annually; needs adequate moisture during the growing season.
Soil Type Fertile, well-drained soil with a pH of 5.8 to 7.0 is ideal.
Key Corn Season Usually planted in April and harvested from July to August.
Fertilizer Recommendations
  • NPK Fertilizer (20-20-20): Enhances overall growth and yield.
  • Urea Fertilizer: Essential for nitrogen supply, especially during the vegetative phase.
Irrigation Regular irrigation is important, especially in the dry season; overhead irrigation is preferred.
Carrot/গাজর - Crop Information
Carrot Cultivation Details in Bangladesh:
Aspect Details
Climate Cool climate, with optimal growth in temperatures between 15°C and 25°C.
Ideal Temperature 16°C to 22°C
Humidity Moderate humidity, with around 60-70% being optimal.
Rainfall 75-150 cm annually, with moderate irrigation required in drier periods.
Soil Type Light, sandy loam soil with good drainage and a pH of 6.0 to 7.0.
Planting Season
  • Winter: October - December
Fertilizer Recommendations
  • NPK Fertilizer (5-10-10): Promotes root development.
  • Organic Matter: Helps improve soil structure.
Irrigation Regular, light irrigation is important for uniform root growth.
Cauliflower/ফুলকপি - Crop Information
Cauliflower Cultivation Details in Bangladesh:
Aspect Details
Climate Cool climate is ideal, with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 20°C for growth.
Ideal Temperature 12°C to 20°C
Humidity Moderate to high humidity is preferred, around 70-80%.
Rainfall 75-150 cm, with irrigation required in dry periods.
Soil Type Well-drained, fertile loamy soil with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.0.
Planting Season
  • Winter: October - February
Fertilizer Recommendations
  • NPK Fertilizer (10-10-10): Balanced growth and head development.
  • Compost: Improves soil organic matter content.
Irrigation Regular irrigation is essential during head formation.

Tomato/টমেটো - Crop Information
Tomato Cultivation Details in Bangladesh:
Aspect Details
Climate Warm with moderate rainfall. Tomatoes grow best in temperatures between 20°C and 25°C.
Ideal Temperature 18°C to 30°C
Humidity Moderate humidity is required, ideally between 50-70%.
Rainfall 80-120 cm annually.
Soil Type Well-drained, fertile loamy soil with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.0.
Planting Season
  • Winter: October - March
  • Summer: April - August
Fertilizer Recommendations
  • NPK Fertilizer (10-10-10): Ensures balanced growth.
  • Compost: Improves soil structure and fertility.
Irrigation Regular watering, especially during flowering and fruit development stages.
